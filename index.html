Air-Blast Atomization: Primary Atomization Study

CFD Adaptive Mesh VOF

This research focuses on the liquid sheet breakup mechanisms in high-speed airflows. Using the Basilisk C solver, we implemented Volume of Fluid (VOF) methods with octree Adaptive Mesh Refinement (AMR) to capture interface dynamics at scales previously computationally prohibitive.

Resulting Impact

Improved understanding of droplet size distribution leading to more efficient fuel injection systems in aerospace applications.

HVAC System Optimization

SolidWorks Thermal Analysis Optimization

Developed optimized ducting layouts for commercial complexes to reduce static pressure losses. Performed Finite Element Analysis (FEA) on structural mounts and thermal expansion joints to ensure long-term reliability under variable load conditions.

Resulting Impact

Reduced system energy consumption by 14% through improved aerodynamic routing of main airflow arteries.
